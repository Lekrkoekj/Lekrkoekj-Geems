History

Lekrkoekj Geems is a Dutch website made by Lekrkoekj on the 12th of December, 2017 to be able to play unblocked Flash games at school. Later on, Lekrkoekj's own games were added to the website. Because of the recent removal of Adobe Flash Player in web browsers, the site now serves as a place to play all games made by Lekrkoekj.
